Who's the Winner?!

Welcome to Connect 4 with a bit of an EXPLOSION! The rules are simple, but a little different. You or your opponent must achieve 4 kittens or puppies in a row. However, either player has the option to use a CATBOMB to erase the opposing player's last move. Use this wisely, each player only gets 1 CATBOMB per game. Happy playing!
